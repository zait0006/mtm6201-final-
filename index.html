About Me

born in Dubai on June 23, 2004, has always been a creative and ambitious individual. Growing up in the bustling metropolis of Dubai, she was exposed to cultural fusion and the dynamic energy of urban life. Ella had a strong desire to explore the world through multiple lenses, both physical and metaphorical, throughout her childhood. Her scholastic experience began in Dubai, where she studied till grade 9, immersed in a diverse learning environment that extended her perspectives and piqued her intellectual interest. Ella began a new chapter in her life in 2018, moving to Canada in search of fresh chances and experiences.
